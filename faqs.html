Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the warranty on Heatwave hair dryers?

All Heatwave hair dryers come with a 2-year warranty. If you experience any issues, feel free to contact us for support or a replacement.

2. How do I care for my Heatwave hair dryer?

To keep your hair dryer in top condition, make sure to clean the filter regularly and avoid dropping it. Store it in a cool, dry place when not in use.

3. Can I return my Heatwave hair dryer?

Yes, we offer a 30-day return policy for all products. If you're not satisfied, you can return the product for a full refund or exchange.

4. Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries. Shipping fees and delivery times may vary based on your location.
